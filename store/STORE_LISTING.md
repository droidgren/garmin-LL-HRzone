## App Name

Last Lap HR Zone

## Short Description

Shows the average heart rate zone from your last completed lap.

## Full Description

Last Lap HR Zone is a Connect IQ data field for interval-focused training.

It calculates the average heart rate of your most recently completed lap and shows that result as a decimal zone value (for example, `3.4` = 40% through zone 3).

### Highlights

- Uses your heart rate zone configuration from your Garmin profile
- Updates immediately after each lap event (manual or auto-lap)
- Shows `--` until your first lap is completed
- Optional Garmin-style HR zone digit colors with an on-device toggle (default ON)
- Resets cleanly when the activity timer is reset

### Typical Use Cases

- Track lap intensity consistency during interval sessions
- Compare recovery lap versus work lap effort
- Keep pacing and effort aligned with target training zones
